# Heroines of Vietnam: Stories of Courage

An interactive storytelling website about the courageous women who shaped Vietnam's history.

## Project Overview

This is a scroll-based storytelling website inspired by NBC News specials, featuring parallax effects, interactive elements, and cinematic design. The project tells the story of Vietnamese heroines across five sections:

1. **Not Forgotten** - Hero section with parallax effect
2. **The Spirit of Rebellion** - Hai Bà Trưng & Lady Triệu
3. **Faces of Defiance** - Võ Thị Sáu & Nguyễn Thị Định
4. **Beyond the Battlefield** - The Unsung Sisterhood
5. **Her Strength Today** - Legacy and Reflection

## Features

- **Parallax scrolling** effects on hero section
- **Interactive hover cards** for heroines
- **Modal pop-ups** for detailed stories
- **Horizontal gallery slider** for resistance fighters
- **Auto-fading slideshow** for archival photos
- **Modern cards** with hover effects
- **Smooth scroll animations**
- **Responsive design**
